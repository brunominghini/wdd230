Welcome to Cozumel

Escape the touristy western part of the island and explore the vast coasts and inlets on the southern and eastern shores with our rental services!

About Us

A motor scooter rental company located on the island of Cozumel, catering to locals, tourists, enthusiasts, and cruise-based touring groups.

We offer friendly and easy-to-rent services for motor scooters, jeeps, and side-by-side ATVs.

Our Locations

We have two physical rental locations on Cozumel:

  • Near the Playa del Carmen-Cozumel Ferry dock in el Centro de Cozumel.
  • Near the Terminal Puerta Maya further south along the coast, catering to cruise lines and patrons.

Our Services

We provide rentals for motor scooters, jeeps, and side-by-side ATVs, aiming to offer hassle-free and enjoyable experiences for our customers.
